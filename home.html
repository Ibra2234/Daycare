About

Welcome to My Daycare! We are a home-based daycare, operated and run by a loving mom, located in Bristow, Virginia that provides high-quality care for children ages One to Nine. Our goal is to create a warm and nurturing environment where children can learn, grow, and thrive. We believe that children deserve the best care, and that's why we make sure that our daycare feels like a second home for your child, filled with love and care, and staffed with loving and experienced caregivers.

Our daycare offers a variety of programs and activities that are designed to meet the needs of each child. We provide a structured daily schedule that includes activities such as art, music, story time, and outdoor play. We believe that children learn best through play, that's why we provide opportunities for children to engage in creative, hands-on activities that promote exploration, experimentation, and self-expression. We also provide healthy meals and snacks throughout the day, to ensure that your child is well-nourished and ready to learn and grow. Services

At My Daycare, we understand the importance of providing high-quality care for your little ones. That's why we offer both full-time and part-time care options for children ages One to Nine. Our daily schedule is designed to provide a balance of structured activities, such as art and music, and free playtime. This allows children to explore their creativity and learn through hands-on experiences, while also providing opportunities for unstructured play and social interaction.

We understand the importance of nutrition for children's growth and development, which is why we provide healthy meals and snacks throughout the day. Our menu is designed with the help of a nutritionist, and we make sure to cater to different dietary needs, so your child gets all the essential nutrients they need to thrive.
